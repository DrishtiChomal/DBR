How to improve your DBR
1
Clear your smallest loans first — reduces monthly obligations fastest and improves DBR immediately.
2
Cancel credit cards you do not use — unused limits still count against your DBR in some bank assessments.
3
Extend your existing mortgage term — reduces monthly payment and frees up DBR capacity.
4
Consolidate multiple loans into one lower monthly payment before applying.
5
Add a co-borrower — splits the DBR calculation across two incomes and doubles your capacity.
